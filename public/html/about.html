WHO WE ARE

About

My name is Christian Brooks. Me and wife got married in the Spring of 2020, right in the midst of COVID-19. When I started dating my now wife, I realized I need to get a career to support a family with her one day. I decided to become a web developer, and went to UNC Charlotte’s Full Stack Web Dev Bootcamp where I learned everything I need to know to make enterprise level websites, with the latest technologies and newest standards. After developing websites at fortune 500 companies for a few years I realized I had the skills and capability to make a difference for small businesses. So I got to work figuring out what small businesses need, and I found out they need fast, reliable websites, for an affordable price that won’t gouge their profit margins. On top of running a business, they don’t need the stress of trying to keep up with a website. That’s where I wanted to relieve the stress, so owners can put that responsibility on me, and focus on running their business.
