WHAT WE DO

At Brooks Web Design we craft enterprise level sites and put them in the hands of small business owners all over the United States. We build static sites with 100% hand-written code and styles resulting in high performance. With higher performance, comes more customers, which means more money in your pocket.

Mobile First Design

40% of consumers will abandon a site after 3 seconds of waiting for a site to load. We build your website starting with mobile designs first, and then add on to this design for tablets and desktops. This increases performance for your customers.

Responsive Web Layouts

With responsive web designs we style the site to work with any screen size. Your website will look good on every screen size from an iphone, an ipad, or even wide screen monitor!

Mobile Site Optimization

60% of web traffic is on mobile, yet most template sites prioritize desktop layout, instead we optimize your site to run on mobile as fast as possible, which ranks websites higher on google.
