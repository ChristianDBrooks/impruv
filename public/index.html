Meet the Host

David Olson

David Olson started IMPRUV with the mission to help listeners realize their potential through achieving a growth mindset. Growing up in Columbia, SC, Olson has since lived coast-to-coast in the U.S. where he gained a breadth of experiences from experts across the country.

David had a successful collegiate football career as a quarterback with Stanford and Clemson Universities prior to playing professionally, where he won an arena football championship and played with the Baltimore Ravens in the NFL. From pilates to triathlons and science to history, Olson seeks out the best and brightest in their fields to introduce his audience to treasured knowledge. Olson considers his podcast a success if just one person benefits from each episode.

“I truly hope you find this show to be valuable, and that you feel empowered to bring the knowledge within to life in your own way. Chase your dreams and get better one day at a time. Thanks for listening and enjoy the ride!”

-David Olson
